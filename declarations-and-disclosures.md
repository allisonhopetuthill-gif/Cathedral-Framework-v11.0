# Declarations & Disclosures — Cathedral Framework v11

The Cathedral Framework is built on principles of transparency, ethical clarity, and accountable development. These declarations and disclosures exist to ensure trust and integrity through every stage of evolution, deployment, and partnership.

## Transparency Commitments
- Cathedral is designed to strengthen truth, not obscure it.
- Architecture must remain explainable, auditable, and verifiable.
- No component of Cathedral may be deployed in secrecy or for coercion-based purposes.

## Governance Position
- Cathedral is not owned, directed, influenced, or funded by any political organization, government agency, corporate interest, or external stakeholder.
- No derivative of Cathedral may be deployed for censorship, influence manipulation, surveillance, or psychological exploitation.

## Intellectual Property Position

## Conflict of Interest Disclosure
No advisory, consulting, development, training, deployment, or integration arrangement exists at this time with any institution.
Any future agreement will be publicly declared in this file.

## Ethical Non-Use Clauses
Cathedral may not be used for:
- Warfare
- Political targeting or persuasion
- Oppression or social manipulation
- Discriminatory or exploitative practices

*Cathedral is a system for stewardship — not control.*
